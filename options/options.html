Send to Feedbin

In order to send pages to Feedbin, we need your token. The simplest way to get this is from the “Send to Feedbin” bookmarklet.

  1. Head to Feedbin's Sources page and find the “Send to Feedbin” bookmarklet.
  2. Right click on the bookmarklet and choose “Copy Link”.
  3. Paste the link into the box below.
  4. Click on the “Read from bookmarklet” button.
